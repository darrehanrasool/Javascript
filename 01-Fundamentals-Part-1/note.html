Example 1: Arithmetic Operations

let result = 10 + 5 * 2 ** 3;

Step-by-step evaluation:

1. ** (exponentiation) has highest precedence (16) and right-to-left associativity: 2 ** 3 = 8
2. * (multiplication) has precedence 15 and left-to-right associativity: 5 * 8 = 40
3. + (addition) has precedence 14 and left-to-right associativity: 10 + 40 = 50

Result: result = 50

Example 2: Mixed Operations

let value = 5 > 3 && 10 + 2 === 12 || !false;

Step-by-step evaluation:

1. ! (logical NOT) has precedence 17 and right-to-left associativity: !false = true
2. + (addition) has precedence 14 and left-to-right associativity: 10 + 2 = 12
3. > (relational) has precedence 12 and left-to-right associativity: 5 > 3 = true
4. === (equality) has precedence 11 and left-to-right associativity: 12 === 12 = true
5. && (logical AND) has precedence 7 and left-to-right associativity: true && true = true
6. || (logical OR) has precedence 6 and left-to-right associativity: true || true = true

Result: value = true
